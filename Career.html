Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) Specialist Career

AI and ML jobs are in demand today and they are certainly the best tech jobs for the future. Reports from various sources have highlighted the growth rate in demand and salary in the domain. AI and ML specialists are involved in developing systems that can process information like humans and make intelligent decisions.
These systems have found use in speech recognition, natural language processing, automated stock trading, fraud detection in banking and finance, security, home automation and innumerable applications in various industries. ML models use data from various sources that are relevant to the problem. They are then trained to recognise a pattern in the data. With the availability of immense data and computing power, ML is finding innovative applications even in our daily lives like recommender systems in streaming services and e-commerce.





Digital Marketing Specialist Career

Promoting businesses, reaching potential customers and converting them into paying customers for profitability is the primary goal of a for-profit company. The process through which companies achieve this has changed a lot over the years. It’s still evolving and digital marketing is at the centre of this change. A digital marketer capitalizes on the reach of social media and other digital media channels to achieve the business goals of a company.
A digital marketing specialist and the team members use their knowledge of organic and paid marketing on social media channels, search engine results and other web properties to reach potential customers. They create a marketing mix that engages potential customers, adds value to their lives and converts them as paid customers.



Management Career

In terms of managerial methods and organizational capacities, Indian businesses are unrivaled. Recently, India’s B-schools and colleges have developed an extraordinary amount of managerial talent. With their different skill sets, the partnership between these young managers and seasoned management professionals sets them apart from the rest of the world. A management professional can work in a variety of fields, including marketing, human resources, finance, operations, and logistics. Many of these management positions necessitate an MBA.   • Daily operations
  • Administration
  • Delegation
  • Recruitment
  • Liaising
  • Enforcing policies
  • Training
  • Evaluation
  • Motivating
    • Coaching, organizing, budgeting, technology adaption and flexibility, management structures, communication, project management, teamwork, and initiatives are required. Administrative Officer, Project Manager, Brand Manager, Sales Director, and so on are examples of roles.








    Medical Professionals Career

    Medical technologists may work behind the scenes, but they are as important as medical professionals and as much in demand. They are trained professionals who investigate the patients as advised by the consulting doctor.
    Some of the tests and analyses they are involved in are related to pathology, histology, haematology. They perform tests on patient’s samples and prepare a report for the medical professionals. Medical technologists also work in radiology and optometry.
    As in the medical profession, these non-medical professionals also need extensive training in the area they wish to work in. You must identify the area of interest and complete the training required to get into this field with growth potential in future.
